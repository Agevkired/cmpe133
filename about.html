ProConnect

Founded in 2015, ProConnect connects the world's professionals to make them more productive and successful.

Inspired by Professor Chao-Li Tarng a group of elite software engineering students from San Jose State Univerity teamed up to make the worlds largest professional network

Our mission is simple, connect the world's professionals to make them more productive and successful. When you join ProConnect, you get access to people, news, updates, and insights that help you be great at what you do.
